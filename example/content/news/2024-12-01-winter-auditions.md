We're excited to announce that auditions for our **Spring 2025 Season** are now open! This year's lineup promises to be our most ambitious yet, featuring both beloved classics and exciting contemporary works.

## What We're Looking For

### Singers
We welcome singers of all experience levels and voice types:

- **Soprano** - Both lyric and dramatic roles available
- **Mezzo-Soprano** - Featured roles in our upcoming productions
- **Tenor** - Leading and supporting roles
- **Baritone** - Character roles and ensemble positions
- **Bass** - Strong character roles and chorus opportunities

### Musicians
Our orchestra is growing! We're particularly seeking:

- **Strings** - Violin I & II, Viola, Cello, Double Bass
- **Woodwinds** - Flute, Oboe, Clarinet, Bassoon
- **Brass** - French Horn, Trumpet, Trombone
- **Percussion** - Timpani and general percussion

## Audition Process

### When
- **Initial Auditions**: January 15-17, 2025
- **Callbacks**: January 22-24, 2025

### Where
**Duchy Community Centre**
123 Main Street, Duchy
*Rehearsal Room A*

### What to Prepare

**For Singers:**
1. One aria of your choice (any language, any period)
2. One art song or musical theatre piece
3. Scales and vocal exercises
4. Sheet music for our accompanist

**For Musicians:**
1. A solo piece of your choice (3-5 minutes)
2. Sight-reading exercises will be provided
3. Ensemble pieces for callback auditions

## How to Apply

Ready to join our company? Here's how to get started:

1. **Email us** at [auditions@duchyopera.co.uk](mailto:auditions@duchyopera.co.uk)
2. **Include** your name, voice type/instrument, and brief experience summary
3. **Attach** a recent photo and resume (if available)
4. **Schedule** your audition time - we'll respond within 48 hours

## What Happens Next

After auditions, we'll be in touch within one week with results. Selected performers will join us for our **Spring Intensive Workshop** starting February 1st, leading up to our main productions in April and May.

## Questions?

Don't hesitate to reach out! Contact our Artistic Director, Sarah Mitchell, at [sarah@duchyopera.co.uk](mailto:sarah@duchyopera.co.uk) or call our office at 01234 567890.

We can't wait to meet you and welcome new voices to our Duchy Opera family!
